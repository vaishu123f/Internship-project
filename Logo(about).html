Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in education, research, and innovation, empowering students to become leaders who contribute meaningfully to society and drive positive change in the world.

We envision a future where our graduates are at the forefront of technological advancement, social transformation, and sustainable development, making a lasting impact on communities worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that combines academic rigor with practical skills, preparing students for successful careers and lifelong learning.

To foster a culture of innovation, research, and entrepreneurship that addresses real-world challenges and creates opportunities for growth.

To promote diversity, inclusivity, and ethical values, ensuring every student has equal opportunities to excel and contribute to society.

To build strong partnerships with industry, government, and communities to enhance learning outcomes and create meaningful impact.

About Us

Founded in 1995 Acharya aims to revolutionize education. With over 15,000 students and 50+ academic programs annually, it stands among the global education elite. Located in India's technical hub, Bangalore, Acharya prioritizes innovation and knowledge. The campus is spread across 80-acres and houses a centralized library, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a 10,000+ seating stadium along with academic blocks.
The institution fosters experiential and collaborative learning, shaping well-rounded individuals, evident in its diverse student population from 55+ countries.
