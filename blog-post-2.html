Innovation Takes Center Stage at Annual Tech Fest 2025

The Annual Tech Fest 2025 concluded with resounding success, showcasing groundbreaking projects and innovations from our talented students across various technology domains.

Event Overview

This year's tech fest saw participation from over 500 students who presented 150+ innovative projects spanning Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), Web Development, Mobile Applications, and Emerging Technologies. The three-day event attracted industry leaders, tech enthusiasts, and students from across the country.

Key Highlights

AI & Machine Learning Projects

Students showcased impressive AI projects including a smart healthcare assistant, automated traffic management system, and a personalized learning recommendation engine. The winning project was an AI-powered crop disease detection system that can identify plant diseases with 95% accuracy.

IoT Innovations

IoT projects demonstrated practical applications in smart homes, agriculture, and industrial automation. Notable projects included a smart irrigation system, home automation using voice commands, and an industrial safety monitoring system.

Web Development Showcase

Web development projects featured modern full-stack applications, progressive web apps, and innovative e-commerce solutions. Students demonstrated expertise in React, Node.js, and modern web technologies.

Guest Speakers

The event featured keynote sessions from industry leaders:

  • Dr. Anjali Mehta - Chief Technology Officer, TechCorp India - "Future of AI in India"
  • Rahul Sharma - Senior Software Architect, Google - "Building Scalable Web Applications"
  • Priya Patel - IoT Expert, Microsoft - "IoT Revolution in Smart Cities"

Workshops & Competitions

Hands-on workshops were conducted on topics including:

  • Machine Learning with Python
  • Building RESTful APIs
  • Mobile App Development with Flutter
  • Cloud Computing Fundamentals
  • Cybersecurity Best Practices

Industry Partnerships

This year's tech fest saw increased participation from industry partners, with companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, and startups offering internships and job opportunities to outstanding participants.
