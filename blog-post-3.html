Celebrating Success: Graduation Ceremony 2025

The university celebrated the achievements of over 500 graduates at the annual convocation ceremony, marking a significant milestone in their academic journey.

The grand graduation ceremony was held at the university's main auditorium with over 2,000 attendees including graduates, their families, faculty members, and distinguished guests. The event was graced by the presence of Dr. Ramesh Kumar, renowned educationist and former Vice-Chancellor, as the chief guest.
